# Stellar 2025: A Podium Finish (Year in Review)
**Author:** Gabriella Pellagatti | **Source:** stellar.org

## Privacy-Related

- 2025 strategy included implementing privacy features balancing configurability with compliance
- SDF joined the Confidential Token Association (alongside OpenZeppelin, Zama, Inco)
- X-Ray upgrade (Protocol 25) provides native ZK primitives
- Ecosystem projects: Moonlight, Amon Privacy, human.tech

## Key Metrics

- Real-world assets up 172%
- TVL up 95% to $211M+
- 800 active projects
- Full-time developer growth 31%
- 10M+ active accounts, 21.5B+ total operations

## Institutional Milestones

- Franklin Templeton: $580M+ tokenized U.S. Treasury fund expanded to Europe
- PayPal USD launched on Stellar
- US Bank tested custom stablecoin issuance
- MoneyGram stablecoin app in Colombia
- Marshall Islands: world's first onchain UBI disbursement
