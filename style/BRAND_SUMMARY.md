# Stellar Brand Guidelines 2026 - Summary

## Logos
- Always include the Stellar Monogram
- Two logo variants available (light/dark)

## Typography
- **Lora** (serif): Headlines, subheads, body copy
- **Inter** (sans-serif): Captions, body copy, detailed/smaller text

## Colors

| Name | HEX | RGB | Use |

## Graphics
- Fine-dotted textures
- Fluid gradients
- Subtle noise for depth and atmospheric movement

- Separate variants for light and dark backgrounds
