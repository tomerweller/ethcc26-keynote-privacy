# Meridian 2025: Building the Future of Finance
**Author:** Claire Grant | **Source:** stellar.org

## Privacy-Related Highlights

- **Tomer Weller** (CPO) outlined Stellar's privacy roadmap on the Copacabana Stage
- Strategy: research funding, core infrastructure investment, open-source solutions
- "Blockchains should be open and transparent by default. But privacy is necessary for mass adoption -- configurable and compliance-forward from the start."

## Major Announcements

- Mercado Bitcoin: $200M in tokenized fixed income and equity
- Centrifuge: deRWA products with $20M anchor investment
- Ondo Finance: USDY deployed (yield-bearing tokenized U.S. Treasuries)
- MoneyGram: next-gen app built exclusively on Stellar
- Pantera Capital: committed to investing in Stellar-based entrepreneurs
- Circle: CCTP V2 integration (USDC across 13 chains)
- PayPal PYUSD: live on Stellar
