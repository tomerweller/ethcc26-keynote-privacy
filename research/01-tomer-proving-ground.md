# Stellar: The Proving Ground for Real-World Privacy
**Author:** Tomer Weller | **Source:** stellar.org

## Key Points

- Traditional finance maintains privacy through centralized ledgers but sacrifices transparency and neutrality
- Public blockchains prioritize openness for security and trust, yet enterprises need confidentiality
- Stellar aims to unite these seemingly opposing requirements

## Three Foundational Principles

1. **Open and Transparent by Default** -- Public visibility ensures accountability, auditability, and equal access
2. **Opt-In and Configurable Privacy** -- Privacy as an application-layer feature, not all-or-nothing
3. **Compliance-Ready Architecture** -- "Solutions that ignore illicit finance safeguards cannot be adopted by regulated financial institutions"

## Three-Pronged Strategy

1. **Research & Innovation** -- Funding academic research (Stellar Community Fund, Academic Research Grant Program), backing projects like Moonlight
2. **Core Privacy Infrastructure** -- Partnership with Nethermind to integrate Risc Zero's zkVM verifier into Soroban; confidential token building blocks and ZK proof verification
3. **Open-Source Solutions** -- Confidential Tokens prototypes; joined Confidential Token Association alongside OpenZeppelin and Zama
