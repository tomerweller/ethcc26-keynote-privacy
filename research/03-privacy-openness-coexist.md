# Can Privacy and Openness Coexist?
**Author:** Claire Grant (panel recap) | **Source:** stellar.org

## Panel: Stellar House Miami

Panelists: Shiv Shankar (Boundless CEO), Robinson Burkey (Wormhole co-founder)

## Key Insights

1. **Privacy-Centered Tech Stack** -- Blockchain's transparency actually enables privacy implementation. Shankar: "The unique thing about blockchain is that it allows you to be configurable in what you share and what you don't share." Stack: Stellar (settlement) + Boundless (computation) + Wormhole (cross-chain interop).

2. **Institutional Adoption Drivers** -- Validators being "fully doxxed" and legally liable appeals to traditional finance. Privacy is the primary blocker, not decentralization or technical capacity.

3. **Bottom-Line Economics** -- "Moving assets on crypto is cheaper." Compliance and profitability are the essential metrics driving institutional blockchain adoption.
