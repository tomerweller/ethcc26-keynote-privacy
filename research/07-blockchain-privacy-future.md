# How Blockchain Privacy Will Shape The Next Financial System
**Source:** stellar.org/learn

## Key Quote

"Crypto is the only industry where we are comfortable with neighbors seeing our salaries, savings, and spending."

## Three Payment Models

1. **Web2:** Credit card processors on centralized servers -- privacy but no transparency
2. **Web3:** Smart contract transactions fully visible -- transparency but no privacy
3. **Zero-Knowledge Model:** Privacy pools for anonymous transfers with publicly verifiable transactions -- best of both worlds

## Transformative Outcomes

- Cross-border payments without revealing business relationships
- Consumer transactions with traditional finance-level discretion
- Regulatory compliance through cryptographic verification instead of invasive audits
